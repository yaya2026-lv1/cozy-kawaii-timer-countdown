# 🕰️ Custom Theme Countdown Timer

Welcome to my custom, multi-theme countdown timer website! This is a responsive, ad-free focus utility designed to look beautiful on both desktop monitors and mobile phone screens.

## ✨ Features
* **4 Instant Themes:** Switch between a warm **Cozy Café** vibe, a sleek **Professional** look, a pastel **Cute Kawaii** design, or a bold **Cartoon-ish** comic aesthetic.
* **Custom Time Alerts:** Set your own custom hours, minutes, and seconds.
* **Audio Alerts:** Plays a clear, friendly chime when the countdown hits zero.
* **Fully Responsive:** Perfectly adapts whether you are tracking tasks on your desktop background or checking it on your phone.

## 🚀 How to Use It
1. Open the live website link (provided by GitHub Pages above).
2. Click on your preferred theme button at the top to set your mood.
3. Type in your desired time and hit **Start**.
4. Enjoy your focused, aesthetic time block!
